A Brief Psychohistory of Indian Politics- History Repeats Itself

Nov 29 2014December 1, 2014

With the Maharashtra and Haryana elections, one thing is clear, BJP has got a formula for their election campaign and we will see it in all the coming state elections. This formula has been very successful for the BJP.
Modi is made the face of the election campaign and his popularity is used to cash in votes. “Chalo Modi ke saath” is the common slogan.

This reminds me of Indira Gandhi, where she systematically sidelined all the regional leaders. She was the face of the Congress and people voted for the Congress not even bothering about who the candidate was!
Even though this secured her position in the Congress, it became the fundamental reason for the rise of many regional parties and the era of coalition politics. The current state of Congress is due to the chain of events that was triggered by Indira Gandhi.

What Modi-Shah have adopted is no conspiracy to sideline regional leaders, but just a rash method to win the elections. If the same strategy is used in all the coming elections, history will repeat itself.
